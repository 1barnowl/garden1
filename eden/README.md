An economic sentiment monitor will continuously ingest real-time financial news, market prices, analyst reports, and macroeconomic indicators. It would use natural language processing to extract tone, themes, and directional signals from news headlines, earnings calls, and policy statements. These signals would be aggregated into sentiment scores across asset classes, sectors, and regions to detect shifts in investor confidence and economic outlook. The system would present dashboards and alerts that help analysts and decision-makers anticipate market moves, risk changes, and emerging economic trends.
